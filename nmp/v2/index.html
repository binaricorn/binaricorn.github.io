New life after the Retreat on is still far away. A few women were given the choice of working for a stable wage and preparing for Re-inhabitation while their sisters left for the perilous trek. Maybe they weren't heard from again because they died. Or maybe they were just too happy in their new homes to bother remember the pain of the previous one. Silver-winged panels bring news from the Habitable World Bank system and the day's Outer Conditions. They were told ahead of time, and knew how to react when they woke up. They were given instructions and could only remember those.
